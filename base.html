Diabetes Prevalance and Income per Capita in the United States, 2004-2013


This bivariate map displays personal income per capita (in 2013 dollars) and the prevalance of diagnosed diabetes on a county level. Each color corresponds to counties that fit within a percentile range for either statistic across the 2004-2013 period, where grey represents the lowest 20% of counties in either category, bright blue represents counties in the top 20% of income, and bright red represents counties in the top 20% of diabetes prevalance.

Click on a county to zoom in, click again to zoom out, and hover over counties or the key to show specific data.

Sources: CDC county-level diabetes dataset, U.S. Dept. of Commerce historical data
